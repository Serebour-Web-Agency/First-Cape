Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

1. How We Use Cookies

FirstCape Estate Management uses cookies to:

  • Keep our website secure and functional
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you navigate our site
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Can Be Disabled?
Essential Required for website to function properly Session ❌ No
Analytics Help us understand website usage 26 months ✅ Yes
Preference Remember your settings and choices 1 year ✅ Yes

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off:

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in and secure your session
  • Security cookies: Detect suspicious activity and prevent attacks
  • Load balancing: Distribute traffic evenly across servers

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site:

  • _ga: Distinguishes unique users (2 years)
  • _gid: Distinguishes unique users (24 hours)
  • _gat: Throttles request rate (1 minute)

What we track:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • How you arrived at our site
  • Device and browser information
  • General location (city/country)
  • Property searches and interactions

We do NOT track:

  • ❌ Personal identifying information
  • ❌ Passwords or payment details
  • ❌ Private conversations
  • ❌ Exact physical address

2.3 Preference Cookies

These remember your choices:

  • Search filters: Property type, location, price range preferences
  • Display settings: Grid vs list view, sort order
  • Language: Preferred language (if multilingual site)

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

3.1 Google Services

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis
  • Google Maps: Interactive property location maps
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Cloudflare

  • Purpose: Security and performance (DDoS protection, CDN)
  • Cookies: __cfduid, cf_clearance
  • Privacy Policy: Cloudflare Privacy Policy

4. How to Control Cookies

4.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser:

Google Chrome:

  1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  2. Choose "Block third-party cookies" or "Block all cookies"

Firefox:

  1. Settings → Privacy & Security
  2. Select "Custom" under Enhanced Tracking Protection

Safari:

  1. Preferences → Privacy
  2. Check "Block all cookies"

Microsoft Edge:

  1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  2. Manage and delete cookies

4.2 Opt-Out of Google Analytics

Install Google's opt-out browser add-on:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

4.3 Mobile Devices

iOS (iPhone/iPad):

  • Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies

Android:

  • Chrome app → Settings → Site settings → Cookies
⚠️ Important: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality. Some features may not work properly without cookies enabled.

5. Do Not Track (DNT)

Our website respects Do Not Track browser settings:

  • If DNT is enabled, we will not load analytics tracking
  • Essential cookies will still be used for security
  • Enable DNT in your browser privacy settings

6. Cookie Consent

6.1 How We Obtain Consent

When you first visit our website:

  • You'll see a cookie notice banner (if implemented)
  • You can accept all cookies or customize preferences
  • Continuing to use the site implies consent

6.2 Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw consent at any time by:

  • Deleting cookies through your browser
  • Adjusting cookie preferences (if cookie banner available)
  • Contacting us to request deletion

7. Cookies and Personal Data

Some cookies may contain personal data:

  • Contact form submissions

This data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

8. International Transfers

Some cookies (like Google Analytics) may transfer data internationally:
